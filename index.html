About Us

Artea Corner is a dining establishment located in Bagabag, Sto. Tomas, Isabela. It is known for its cozy and welcoming atmosphere. The ARtea Corner offers a diverse menu,to suit every taste.

The interior of Artea Corner is adorned with tasteful decor and comfortable seating, creating a relaxing ambiance for diners. The staff is known for their friendly and attentive service, ensuring a pleasant dining experience for all guests.

The menu at Artea Corner features a variety of options, including milktea , Ice Coffee, and ect, as well as favorite burgers. They also serve refreshing drinks, including freshly brewed coffee, smoothies, and milkshakes.

In addition to their delectable food and drinks, Artea Corner also offers a comfortable outdoor seating area, perfect for enjoying the pleasant weather or hosting small gatherings. The Artea Corner is location in Bagabag, Sto. Tomas provides a beautiful backdrop of nature, making it an ideal place to unwind and enjoy a delicious drink and food.

Whether you are looking for a simply a place to relax and enjoy good food, Artea Corner in Bagabag, Sto. Tomas, Isabela is an excellent choice.
